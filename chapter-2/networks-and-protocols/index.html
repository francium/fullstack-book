Chapter 2 Networks and Protocols

The purpose of this first chapter is to outline the history of the internet and describe, at a high level, fullstack development. First we will outline the history of the internet and how it came to be. From there we will look at the modern internet and how it became an integral part of modern life. We'll also briefly discuss what the future may hold for the internet. Finally we'll close off this chapter by discussing fullstack development and laying the foundation from which we can build our knowledge in the rest of this book.

Some readers may choose to skip to the end of this chapter if they're not interest in the history of the internet and early computer networks.
