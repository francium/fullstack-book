Preface

This book is about understanding fullstack applications and fullstack application development from the bottom up. It is not a project based book, nor is it a tutorial on fullstack development. Instead is meant to describe the key concepts relevant to fullstack development, explain fundamentals using descriptions and examples, and discuss the practice of fullstack development.

The reader is expected to have prior knowledge and a good level of comfort with programming in at least one language, but multiple language will better. Prior experience developing a backend or fullstack application would be greatly beneficial in providing a foundation on which to build upon while reading this book. Consequently, this book will not be focusing on explaining fundamental of programming, the specific of a language or programming concepts.

What this book instead does is focus on is a thorough, but not exhaustive, overview of fullstack application development. The topic of fullstack development is vast and constantly evolving. However, unlike much of the material one may find on the internet or in other books, this book instead chooses to focus on the key fundamental concepts that transcend the technologies, frameworks and libraries du jour. That being said, for the sake of explanations, relevance and examples, current popular technologies, libraries and frameworks will be mentioned and used in examples as necessary. However, we will make an effort to not rely solely on them for the purposes of getting the concepts we discuss across to you the reader.

Given the vast amount of information available to discuss, some areas may not be covered as deeply as others. Covering everything in a single book is infeasible and would end up yielding a book that would be too large to write or read. Hence, some topics will be covered at higher levels, but we will make an effort to provide necessary pointers and references to external resources for interested readers.
